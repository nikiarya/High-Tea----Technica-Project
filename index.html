Scrumptious Scones

These soft and chewy treats pairs well with clotted cream and jam!

  1. Heat the Magic Oven: Preheat to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix & Mingle: Whisk 2 cups flour, 1/4 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add 1/2 cup cold butter, cut into tiny pieces, and blend until crumbly.
  3. Pour in the Potion: Mix 2/3 cup milk, 1 egg, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (if you’re feeling fancy). Stir into the crumbly mix until just combined. Toss in 1/2 cup raisins or chocolate chips if you like surprises!
  4. Shape the Magic: Turn dough onto a floured surface. Pat it into a 1-inch thick circle and cut into 8 wedges or circles.
  5. Bake & Enjoy: Pop them into the oven for 12-15 minutes until golden. Cool on a rack and enjoy your whimsical treats warm with butter, jam, or clotted cream!

Tea Arrangement

Perfect Works-Everytime Tea

  1. Choose Your Tea: Pick your tea leaves or bag.
  2. Boil Water: Heat fresh, cold water until it reaches a rolling boil for black/oolong teas or just before boiling for green/white teas.
  3. Preheat Vessel: Swirl hot water in your teapot or cup to warm it.
  4. Measure Tea: Use 1 teaspoon of loose leaves per cup or one tea bag.
  5. Add Tea: Place leaves or bag in the teapot or cup.
  6. Pour Water: Pour hot water over the tea.
  7. Steep: Let it steep for:
    • Black tea: 3-5 minutes
    • Green tea: 2-3 minutes
    • White tea: 4-5 minutes
    • Oolong tea: 4-7 minutes
    • Herbal tea: 5-7 minutes
  8. Remove Tea: Take out the leaves or bag.
  9. Taste and Adjust: Add honey, lemon, or milk if desired.
  10. Serve and Enjoy: Pour into your cup and savor!
